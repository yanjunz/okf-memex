# Overview

Andrej Karpathy is a prominent AI researcher and educator. He is known for his work at OpenAI and as Director of AI at Tesla. He creates widely-followed educational content on deep learning and neural networks, including the "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" YouTube series and open-source projects like nanoGPT and makemore.

# Key Facts

- Former Director of AI at Tesla
- Founding member of OpenAI
- Creator of "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" lecture series
- Author of open-source projects: nanoGPT, makemore, micrograd
- Proposer of the [LLM Wiki Pattern](/concepts/llm-wiki-pattern.md) — a pattern for building personal knowledge bases using LLMs
